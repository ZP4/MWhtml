If you’re unsatisfied with products you purchased from MatchWear, you can return your item(s) as follows: Return policy Items purchased can be returned within 20 days of purchase. Returns are only allowed as a whole outfit with the tags and labels must be attached to the product. For the item to be returnable, there can not be any signs of wear or damage. You must have an original receipt or packing slip to complete your return. Items must be unused and unwashed. Shipping costs are not refundable. Note that items purchased at a 3rd party or re-seller must be returned at those locations and are subject to the 3rd party’s return policy. Exchange policy Items purchased can be exchanged within 20 days of purchase. You can either exchange up to only 2 individual items in each outfit or the entire outfit. Exchanges are only allowed to be made with products in the same range. You must have an original receipt or packing slip to complete your return. Items must be unused and unwashed. Shipping costs are not refundable. If there is an issue the product (it’s damaged, dirty, the wrong size, etc.) please contact us at services.matchwear@gmail.com within 20 days of purchase.
STEP 1: Place item back in to the original box. THE ORIGINAL TAG MUST BE ATTACHED.
STEP 2: Put the return label over the original shipping label.
STEP 3: Drop the box off at any UPS access point.
If you have anymore questions, please feel free to contact us at services.matchwear@gmail.com. Because MatchWear strives to build close relationships with our customers.
